LinkedIn Profile Extractor Icons

To create icons for your Chrome extension:

  1. Take screenshots of the icons below
  2. Save them as:
    • icon16.png (16x16 pixels)
    • icon48.png (48x48 pixels)
    • icon128.png (128x128 pixels)
  3. Place them in the extension/icons/ folder

Or right-click each icon and "Save image as..." if your browser supports it.
